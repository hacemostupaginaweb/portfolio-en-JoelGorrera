Who is Joel?

(Some curiosities about me)

The following is a list of some of the things that I consider define me as a person and that can demonstrate my great versatility and efficiency regardless of the field in which I am involved.

avid reader

For me, reading is the most practical and direct way to assimilate knowledge. (as seen in the photo of my books) That's why much of my skills as a web developer come from being self-taught. I tend to go constantly to the Stoic texts as reinforcement and personal guide to face the difficulties and challenges of life. In my opinion, the only way to grow in any field is to try and fail as many times as necessary until we succeed in what we are pursuing.

Athlete

"Mens sana in corpore sano" You cannot have balance without taking equal care of both the intellect and the physical. Which is why I always do my best to be an active person training every day of the week. Big fan of American Bodybuilding, my effort in the gym helps me to be a much more enthusiastic, determined, and effective person. I don't stop when I'm tired, I stop when the job is done.

Professional illustrator

According to the dictionary, an artist is someone who does something very well, and that's the kind of person I aspire to be. Since I was little I have been a great lover of cartoons, comics, and art in general. Much of my studies have been focused on the study of the concepts of design and creation of messages through images; Skill that I extrapolate in my facet of Front-end developer, and also, the main reason for starting to study HTML5 and CSS3. The image that can be seen to the left of this text is a gif drawn by me

Content creator

I am passionate about making something come out of nothing in all its aspects, and one of the results of this has been to learn to use all kinds of image and video editing programs such as "Adobe Photoshop" or "Adobe Premiere"; Programs with which I have created a YouTube channel with more than 1,000 followers and a total of almost 100,000 views. The objective of the channel is to make available to the Hispanic community the reflections and wisdom of great personalities such as Arnold Schwarzzenegger or Jean Claude Van Damme, so I also do a translation job by subtitling their words from English to Spanish.
